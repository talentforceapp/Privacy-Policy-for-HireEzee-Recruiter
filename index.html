Privacy Policy - Recruiters (Schools and Institutions)

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. Data Collected

We collect the following information from recruiting institutions:

  • Institution name and registration details
  • Authorized signatory information
  • Job postings and communication records

2. Purpose of Collection

Data is collected for the following purposes:

  • Account verification
  • Subscription management
  • Legal and regulatory compliance

3. Data Protection

Recruiter data is stored securely and used strictly for platform operations and compliance purposes. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your institutional information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

4. Data Sharing

Your data may be disclosed only in the following circumstances:

  • To government authorities when legally required
  • For internal audits and compliance checks

We do not sell, rent, or share your institutional data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Rights of Recruiters

As a registered recruiter on our platform, you have the right to:

  • Access and update institutional data
  • Request account deletion subject to legal obligations
